Hungarian walnut roll (Beigli)

30m prep / 45m cook / makes 2 loaves


Allergens: Recipe may contain gluten, wheat, tree nut, egg and yeast.


Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 8g sachet dried yeast
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 3 cups plain flour
  • 200g butter, cubed
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 400g walnuts
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 5 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • 1 egg, beaten

Directions


Step 1

Combine 3/4 cup warm milk, 8g sachet dried yeast and 1 tablespoon caster sugar. Set aside for 5 minutes until foamy. Process 3 cups plain flour, 200g butter, cubed and 2 egg yolks in a food processor until breadcrumbs form. Transfer to a bowl.


Step 2

ix in the yeast mixture. Knead until smooth. Place in an oiled bowl. Cover and set aside for 45 minutes. Process 400g walnuts, 1 teaspoon cinnamon and 5 tablespoons caster sugar in a food processor until finely ground. With the motor running, add 1/4 cup warm milk until a smooth paste forms.


Step 3

Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Knead the dough until smooth, then halve. Roll each portion out to a 13cm x 24cm rectangle. Spread with the walnut filling. Roll up. Slightly flatten to about 7cm wide. Place on a lined baking tray. Brush top with 1 egg, beaten. Stand 20 minutes. Bake for 35-40 minutes.
